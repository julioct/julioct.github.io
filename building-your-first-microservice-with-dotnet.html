Avoid the pitfalls of the monolithic hell

Is your team suffering the symptoms of the monolithic hell? Has your code base become so complex it's too hard to understand and modify? Does it take forever to build and deploy your system? Do you just want to know what's the latest in distributed systems architecture?

Should my teams use microservices?

It may time to move away from the monolith if:

  • Can't scale your system fast enough when needed
  • It takes forever to build, test and deploy
  • Even a small bug can break the entire system
  • The big code base is too hard to understand
