Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 1/25/2024

Welcome to Language Translator. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the information you provide while using our text translation app, available on the Windows Store.

1. Information We Collect

Our app provides text translation services, allowing users to translate text into a chosen language. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The text you enter for translation is processed temporarily and is not stored after the translation process is completed.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no usage of such data. The text entered for translation is used solely for the purpose of providing the translation service.

3. Data Sharing

We do not store or share any data with third parties as no personal data is collected through our app.

4. Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of our app. However, as we do not collect or store any personal information, there are no risks associated with data storage or security.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at julioc@dotnetacademy.io.
