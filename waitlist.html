External Integrations

Integrating with third-party APIs, webhooks, and handling the complete lifecycle reliably

Handling Failures

Preventing duplicate operations and ensuring requests can be safely retried

Async Processing

Data Consistency

Coordinating transactions across databases and message queues reliably
